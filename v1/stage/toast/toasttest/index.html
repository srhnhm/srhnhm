About

Toast Expo is the second installment in an annual festival concerning food+drink as a creativity medium. This year, we are inviting the makers of bread, jam, and toast-inspired works to gather and exchange.

The Toast Expo seeks:

  • Makers of bread

  • Makers of jam

  • Owners or operators of toasters

  • Artists whose work derives from toast or toasters

  • Toast scholars

Program

Symposia of Bread + Jam Makers:
Open sharing and discourse of all theory, practice, research, and experience regarding the production and consumption of toast. Bread and Jam makers are invited to present and share their techniques and wares in an open-floor format.

Presentation of White Papers:
Oral presentations of an opinion on toast with the possibility of audience discussion. These should be submitted individually, and the program committee will organize them into topically coherent sessions, depending on the number of symposia and available space.
