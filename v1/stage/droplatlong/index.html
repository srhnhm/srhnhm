Tweets mentioning colors are plotted on their coordinates of origin. Inspired by the watercolor map tiles released by Stamen Design, each tweet is marked with a colored ink droplet that seeps into place. Why "paint the town red", literally? We were curious about how perceptual qualities - like colors - fit in to the digital/physical in-between of geolocated datapoints. Built with Modest Maps and d3.
